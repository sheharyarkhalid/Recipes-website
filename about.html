Who Are We?

In 1998, a visionary named M. Mirza set out to create a line of ethnic snack foods that would combine traditional and modern flavors. The snacks had to be special, unlike anything else on the market and so this Foods Ltd was born.

With a strong focus on fusion flavors, this Foods became a pioneer in the snack foods industry, offering unique and irresistible flavors that catered to the mass Western market. Their Samosas, Empanadas, and Naan fusion flavors became instant hits with people all over Canada. And they continue to push the boundaries of snack food innovation, coming up with new and exciting flavors for every palate.

Overtime, this Foods has become a household name in napal with their snacks being sold in major grocery chains like Walmart, Sobeys, and Loblaws focusing mainly on locations in napal.
